LAUREL


Backbone - What made her who she is?

Laurel was born and raised in Atlanta, Georgia. She is a big family girl, and enjoys the many traditions she has with her family. One of them being that they set up the Christmas tree the day of Thanksgiving! Her family also enjoys taking care of pets, so Laurel has always lived her life having a cat in the house. One of her most memorable times with her family was when her parents took her to New York for the first time this past December!


Heart - What does she love?

Over Laurel's entire life, friends have had a big part in making every year a great one! She is a very friendly person, and any friend of her's is very grateful to have her. She has a very empathetic heart and will always have her friend's backs when they need her. Along with friends, both swimming and music hold a very special place in her heart. She has swam in many competitions, and winning quite a few! These competitions have even gone regional! As for music, she is into both creating her own music through piano, guitar, and trombone; and listening to music.


Mind - What are her goals?

When Laurel thinks about her future and dreams, many things come to mind. One place that she has always wanted to travel is to Greece. The beautiful buildings, interesting history, and oceans are just a few of the reasons she is craving to travel there. Her dream job is to become a part of something related to design. This can be through software, for example. Also, one of her goals in life is to make a change in the world by helping the environment.
